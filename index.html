... About Relocation Costs
The first 20km are free. If the location where you want photos/videos is over 20km, then there will be travel costs of €0.40 per km. During a photo/video shoot, you can choose to go to multiple locations. Some packs include multiple locations. If 1 location is included, we will discuss it together. If 2 or more locations are included, we will agree on several locations to take different photos/videos (this must be within 10 km of the first agreed location). If it's further than 10km, travel costs will be added again - €0.40 per km. With the video - Max & Ultra packs, travel costs will only be charged if it's further than 30km. About Photo/Video Packs
I put together the packs on my website myself. If you want something else, feel free to contact me, and I will make a custom quote. About Video/Photo - Rolling Shots
If you want rolling shots in the cheaper packs, I will ask for €20 once, and then €10 per rolling shot. Rolling shots are included in the more expensive packs. If you would like more information about rolling shots, be sure to contact me!
