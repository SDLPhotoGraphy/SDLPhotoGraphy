... About Relocation costs.
The first 20km are free. If the location where you want photos/videos is over 20km, there will be travel costs. This is €0.40 per Km. During a photo/video shoot, you can choose to go to multiple locations. Some packs include multiple locations. If 1 location is included, this is the location we discuss together. If 2 or more locations are included, we agree on several locations to take different photos/videos (this must be within 10 km of the first agreed location). If this is further than 10km, travel costs will be added again (€0.40). With the video - max&ultra packs, travel costs will only be charged if it's further than 30km. About Photo/Video Packs.
I put together the Packs on my website myself. Suppose you want something else, feel free to contact me and I will make a quote. About Video/Photo - Rolling shots.
If you want rolling shots in the cheaper packs, I will ask for €20 once, and then €10 per rolling shot. Rolling shots are included in the more expensive packs. If you would like more information about rolling shots, be sure to contact me!
